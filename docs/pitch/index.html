How Does It Work?

Architect's Role in Maximizing Decision-Making Capabilities.

Architect aims to:

  • Minimize Development
  • Maximize User Benefit

Through a comprehensive weighing system within Slack, students and staff will enhance communication through effective question-answer coupling.

***

We call these weights DROPS.

Why Is This Needed?

An invisible companion to all Make Schoolers.

Architect embraces a few core beliefs:

  • People should be empowered to make decisions that affect the community.
  • People shouldn't be held back by organizational structure or hierarchy to act.
  • People should reduce clutter in having their questions answered.
